What Issues Can Be Addressed With Invisalign?
There are many benefits to choosing Invisalign aligners. We provide Invisalign near you to treat various orthodontic concerns,

which include:

Overbite, Underbite, Crowding of the teeth, Large gaps, Unevenly spaced teeth

If you are interested in receiving Invisalign in Dental Works Airdrie, call us to schedule a consultation. We take pride in providing exceptional services to improve our patient’s overall oral health. During a consultation, your dentist will come up with a treatment plan that is catered to your needs. What are the Benefits of Receiving Invisalign?
Comfort and convenience are the biggest benefits of choosing Invisalign for your orthodontic needs.There are many other benefits,

which include:

Brow lift ,Treats and reduces headaches ,Refining a gummy or asymmetrical smile ,Reduces jaw clenching and oversized jaw muscles

What to Expect When Receiving Invisalign in Willow park? Consultation

During a consultation, our doctor will meet with you to discuss the best options for your teeth and determine whether you are a good candidate for Invisalign.

Customization
Once your doctor has determined that you are a good candidate for Invisalign, they will take impressions and X-rays of your teeth so that they can create your custom aligners.
Treatment
You will receive a series of customized aligners to wear during your treatment. These aligners will be worn on a daily basis and only removed when brushing your teeth or eating.
Progress
A new set of aligners will be given to you at certain intervals during your treatment. Your progress will be measured through regularly scheduled checkups.
Completion Once you receive and start wearing your custom aligners, your teeth will start to shift into their new positions. Contact us today to schedule a consultation for Invisalign near you!
