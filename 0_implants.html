Why Choose Dental Implants?
Many patients often get dental implants in Willow Park for a number of reasons. Some causes of tooth loss

include:

Teeth grinding , Injury , Tooth decay , Oral disease

If you are missing any teeth, call us to see if you are the right candidate for dental implants. To be considered a good candidate, you must have healthy gums and a strong jaw bone in order to have a successful implant. During a consultation, yourbdentist will examine your teeth and gums to determine if you can move forward with this treatment.

If you have any questions about receiving dental implants, please feel free to contact us.


How Do Dental Implants Work?

A dental implant is composed of 3 parts: The implant screw, abutment, and the manufactured crown.

The implant screw is surgically implanted in your jawbone and acts as a replacement for the root of the tooth. The abutment is what joins the implanted screw to the manufactured crown. The crown looks just a natural tooth and is installed on the abutment. Once everything is healed, the dental implant looks and feels just like a natural tooth. Dental implants are also just as strong as a natural tooth!

If a dentist determines that you are a good candidate for dental implants in Dental Works Airdrie, your dentist will come up with a treatment plan to decide the best way to place them. Our experienced and professional dentists perform a guided surgery that is a non-invasive procedure. We want to make sure you feel confident going into any procedure with our dental team. Call us to book a consultation for dental implants at Dental Works Airdire. We are more than happy to assist you with any questions you may have.

What to Expect When Receiving Invisalign in Willow park? Consultation

During a consultation, our doctor will meet with you to discuss the best options for your teeth and determine whether you are a good candidate for Invisalign.

Customization
Once your doctor has determined that you are a good candidate for Invisalign, they will take impressions and X-rays of your teeth so that they can create your custom aligners.
Treatment
You will receive a series of customized aligners to wear during your treatment. These aligners will be worn on a daily basis and only removed when brushing your teeth or eating.
Progress
A new set of aligners will be given to you at certain intervals during your treatment. Your progress will be measured through regularly scheduled checkups.
Completion Once you receive and start wearing your custom aligners, your teeth will start to shift into their new positions. Contact us today to schedule a consultation for Invisalign near you!
