There are many reasons why a tooth may need to be extracted. Some reasons included tooth decay, gum disease or a tooth that simply can not be repaired through procedures such as root canal therapy. At our dental clinic in Airdrie, tooth extraction is usually not our first option when dealing with a dental issue. Wisdom teeth are usually extracted before or after they have grown in. If your wisdom teeth cause pain, show signs of decay or have an infection, it is important to visit your dentist near you to talk about getting them extracted.
